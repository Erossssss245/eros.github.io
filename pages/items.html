Items & Blocks

This page lists every custom item and block added by the mod, along with their properties and how to obtain them.

💎 Raw Materials

These are base resources you must gather before crafting advanced mod items.

💎

Luminite Crystal

Found in Crystal Caverns below Y=20. Used in all advanced recipes.

Mining
🌑

Shadow Essence

Dropped by Shadow Walkers. Required for dark-tier equipment.

Mob Drop
🪨

Ancient Stone

Found in Ancient Ruins. Used for structural and decorative blocks.

Structure Loot
🌿

Verdant Fiber

Harvested from Verdant Forest plants. Used in cloth and rope recipes.

Plant
✨

Luminite Dust

Dropped by Glowbeetles or obtained by grinding Luminite Crystals.

Crafting
🔮

Core Shard

Dropped by The Archon boss. Needed for the most powerful end-game items.

Boss Drop

🎒 Items

Item Obtain Use
Archon Rune Crafted (see Recipes) Used to summon The Archon boss
Luminite Lens Crafted Allows night vision when held in off-hand
Shadow Cloak Crafted Grants temporary invisibility on use (30 s cooldown)
Crystal Compass Crafted Points toward the nearest Crystal Cavern entrance
Verdant Scroll Crafted / Loot Teaches you new crafting recipes when used

🧱 Blocks

Block Obtain Properties
Luminite Ore World gen (Y < 20) Hardness 4.5, drops 1–3 Luminite Crystals
Crystal Block Crafted (9× Luminite Crystal) Emits light level 10, full-solid
Mod Workbench Crafted (see Recipes) Opens the mod-specific crafting UI
Ancient Stone Brick Crafted from Ancient Stone Hardness 6.0, blast resistant
Shadow Lantern Crafted Decorative; emits dim light with a purple tint
Verdant Log Verdant Forest New wood type; all plank/slab/stair variants available

⚔️ Tools & Weapons

⚔️

Luminite Sword

8 damage. Applies "Glow" effect on hit, making targets visible through walls for 5 s.

Weapon
⛏️

Luminite Pickaxe

Harvests at diamond speed. Can mine Shadow Stone blocks.

Tool
🗡️

Shadow Dagger

5 damage but grants a 1.5× speed bonus for 3 s after each hit.

Weapon
🏹

Crystal Bow

Fires explosive bolts of Luminite. Consumes Luminite Dust as ammo.

Ranged
🛡️

Archon Shield

8 armor points. Negates all knockback while blocking.

Armor
🔧

Verdant Axe

Chops 3× faster in Verdant Forest biomes. Standard axe stats elsewhere.

Tool

🔨 Crafting Recipes

Below are the crafting recipes for key mod items. All recipes use the vanilla Crafting Table unless noted as Mod Workbench.

ℹ️ Note Install Just Enough Items (JEI) or Roughly Enough Items (REI) to see all in-game recipes directly in your inventory.
